Saturdays.AI - Sevilla

AI Saturdays Sevilla is part of the #AISaturdays movement, a global non-profit initiative with the mission of making AI accessible to anyone making projects with social impact and working collaboratively.

When: 11 Saturdays from 14th March, 2020
Where: Espacio RES

Price: €157*

Experience our AI community in Sevilla

Sevilla has the largest historical center in Spain and one of the three largest in Europe. It swims between deep-rooted traditions and the awakening of a European and cosmopolitan city, accompanied by an innovative ecosystem in growth. Do yo want to be part of it?
